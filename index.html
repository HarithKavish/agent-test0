Fun Facts About Dolphins

Intelligent Creatures

Dolphins have highly developed brains, capable of complex behaviors and problem-solving. They use tools, communicate with each other, and even recognize individual humans.

Marine Masters

Dolphins are highly social animals that live in pods, often traveling long distances together. Their echolocation helps them navigate underwater environments.

Long Lifespan

Wild dolphins can live up to 40-50 years, while some in captivity have lived even longer. Their longevity is a testament to their robust health.
